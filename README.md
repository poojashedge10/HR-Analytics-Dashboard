# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive **HR Analytics Dashboard** built using Power BI to analyze employee attrition, workforce distribution, and key HR metrics. The goal is to identify factors influencing employee turnover and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze employee attrition trends
- Identify high-risk departments and job roles
- Understand the impact of salary and experience on attrition
- Provide actionable insights for HR teams

---

## 📂 Dataset
- Source: IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition Dataset
- Records: ~1470 employees
- Features:
  - Age
  - Gender
  - Department
  - Job Role
  - Monthly Income
  - Years at Company
  - Attrition

---

## 📊 Key KPIs
- Total Employees
- Attrition Count
- Attrition Rate (%)
- Average Salary
- Average Years at Company
- Gender Distribution

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Workforce Overview
- Employee distribution by department
- Gender distribution

### 🔹 Attrition Analysis
- Attrition by department
- Attrition by job role
- Attrition trend by experience

### 🔹 Salary & Experience Insights
- Salary vs attrition (scatter plot)
- Experience vs attrition

---

## 🔍 Advanced Features
- Drill-through (Department → Employee Details)
- Interactive slicers (Department, Job Role, Gender)
- KPI cards with insights
- Clean and professional UI design

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

---

## 📌 Key Insights
- Higher attrition observed in specific departments
- Employees with lower experience tend to leave more
- Salary plays a significant role in employee retention

---

## 🚀 How to Use
1. Open the `.pbix` file in Power BI Desktop
2. Interact with filters and slicers
3. Right-click on visuals to use drill-through feature

---

## 📌 Conclusion
This dashboard helps HR teams monitor workforce trends and make strategic decisions to reduce employee attrition.
